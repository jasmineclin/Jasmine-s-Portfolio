Recent Blender Creations

Mid-Century Modern Dresser


A mid-century modern dresser modeled in Blender, featuring tree wood textures, arched glass-panel doors, and gold hardware. Designed with cubes, cylinders, and subtle lighting to emphasize material detail and form. This image was taken within the Blender's in-built camera function. I used free textures that I found online to help with the realistic look of this asset.


I'm currently in the process of creating my own furniture asset pack to use when I design full isometric rooms in blender. This asset pack would include chairs, tables, beds, dressers, plants, and many other items that help make a room feel like a home. I take inspiration and references from my own Pinterest board, personal taste, and some design shows. What I love about building your own assets is the fact that I'm able to add as much detail as I want to and be able to import my work into a bigger build that I'm working on.
Stay tuned on my Blender journey!

Isometric Loft Bedroom

A cozy loft-style interior modeled in Blender, featuring a split-level design with detailed cabinetry, a staircase, and natural lighting elements. Rendered in grayscale to highlight form and structure.
